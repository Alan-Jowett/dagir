◆ k_label

constexpr std::string_view dagir::ir_attrs::k_label {"label"}
inlineconstexpr

Primary label for nodes or edges.

Intended semantics: a short, human-readable label representing the node or edge. Backends should use this for the visible text label. For GraphViz this maps to label (quoted string); for JSON backends it may be emitted as the label property. If absent, backends may fall back to an id-based label or an empty label.
